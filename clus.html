KUMASI TECHNICAL UNIVERSITY GIVES FURNITURE TO CLUSTER OF STUDENTS

Kumasi Technical University (KsTU) donated classroom furniture to some selected Schools in the Ashanti
Region on Saturday, 28th January 2022. The furniture comprised a set of 322 tables and chairs built with a
combination of solid wood and metal. The support was provided under the kind auspices of the Management of
the University to help address some infrastructure problems facing the schools. The beneficiary schools are
Sakafia Islamic Senior High School, Asokore Mampong, and Juaben Anglican JHS situated at Juaben. Sakafia
Islamic SHS received 180 mono desks from the University to support its furniture needs whiles Juaben Anglican
JHS also received 142 desks to ensure that most of the student population of the school sits comfortably in their
classrooms for effective teaching and learning. On receiving the pieces of furniture, authorities of these schools
commended KsTU for the kind assistance. They described the donation as a great relief to the board, management,
and staff and especially to the students of the school.
